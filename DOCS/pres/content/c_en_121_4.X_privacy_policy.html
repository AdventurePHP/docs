2. Your rights

You have the right to request all information about your PII data at any time. This includes where the information has been requested and/or collected, who is using your data, and for which reason, your data is used. Further, you have the right to request correction, blocking, and deletion of all data.

You have the right to revoke your consent on data processing at any time. The legality of the data processed until revocation remains unaffected.

In case of questions on data privacy, please send your inquiry to above address.

In case of any complaints, plese refer to the respective supervisory authority: https://www.bfdi.bund.de.

3. Data handling

3.1. PII data

With the sign-up for Forum, Wiki, and Issue Management Tool, PII data are requested to allow log-in and participation with he framework community. The PII data can be reviewed and corrected within your profile area at any time. Your data are strictly managed within our systems and are not provided to third parties!

3.2. Server log files

When accessing our website, your browser automatically transmits data to our web servers for technical reasons. With each request, the date and time of access, the URL of the referring web page, the requested file, the amount of transferred data, the browser type and version, the operating system of your computer and IP address are submitted. All data transmitted only for technical reasons evaluated exclusively for statistical purposes, stored, and subsequently deleted. Assigning data to a certain person is not possible for us and does not take place.

3.3. Cookies

Technical cookies are required to conduct operation of the web site. This includes the Online Documentation, the Forum, the Wiki, and the Issue Management Tool. None of the cookies contains PII data.

3.4. Google Analytics

We are using Google Analytics to analyze web site traffic and improve the page constantly. With the web tracking implement, no PII data are stored in Google Analytics.

All data collected by Google can be read about under Help for Google Analytics - Data Privacy.

In case you don't want Google to collect and process web site statistics (including IP address) you can download and install a browser plug-in provided by Google. Please find more information under https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout?hl=en.
