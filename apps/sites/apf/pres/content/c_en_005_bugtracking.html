Bug reports are currently addressed within the forum, because the APF does not have an explicit bugtracking tool at the moment. Due to the fact, that there is no a TRAC or JIRA installation available at the moment, bugtracking was transfered to the forum. The section Bugtracking was introduced to give the opportunity to report or search for bugs. Please refer to the english language APF support forum for more details. Note, that the current entries are in German language only, but do you have the possibility to post reports in englisch language as well.
